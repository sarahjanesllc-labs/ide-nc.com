Custom Machine Design

IDE has experience designing custom one-of-kind machines tailored to a specific application or process.  Typically a client will have a need for a unique machine to perform in a laboratory or production environment.  IDE will generate a specification or statement of work to define the operational goal of the machine.  These custom machines are then designed, built, tested, and delivered to client companies.
